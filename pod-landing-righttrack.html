Our Information Disclosure

Liberty Mutual Insurance - Information Disclosure

We access consumer report information such as claims histories, driving records, and credit-based insurance scores where permitted by law to determine the price you pay for insurance coverage. We may also use this information to provide you with a quote for another line of personal insurance during the auto quote process if requested.

Your insurance score uses information from your credit report to help predict how often you're likely to file a claim, and how expensive those claims will be. When we access your credit-based insurance score, it has no impact on your credit rating.

Both your Social Security Number and insurance score are kept confidential and secure, and will not be resold or redistributed.

We may also submit to an authorized third party to obtain and pre-fill information about your vehicle(s), and prior insurance coverage for you to confirm when you complete the quote request application.
